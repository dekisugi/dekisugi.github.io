## openSUSE Leap 15.1 へのインストール

    cd fxxk
    git clone https://github.com/dekisugi/plasma-workspace.git
    ln -s fxxk/plasma-workspace/applets/digital-clock/package/ ~/.local/share/plasma/plasmoids/org.kde.plasma.digitalclock
